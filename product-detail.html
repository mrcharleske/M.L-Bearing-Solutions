Product Description

The Deep Groove Ball Bearing 6205 is a versatile, high-performance bearing designed for a wide range of industrial applications. This bearing features a simple, robust design that can accommodate radial loads and moderate axial loads in both directions.

Manufactured using premium quality steel and precision engineering processes, this bearing offers excellent durability, low friction, and smooth operation. The deep groove design provides optimal load distribution and extended service life.
