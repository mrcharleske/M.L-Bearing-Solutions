Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the bearing industry in East Africa, M.L Bearing Solutions has grown from a small local supplier to the region's most trusted bearing company.

Our journey began when our founder recognized the critical need for high-quality, reliable bearings in Kenya's rapidly growing industrial sector. What started as a mission to serve local manufacturers has expanded into a comprehensive solution provider serving all 47 counties.

Today, we're proud to be Africa's premier bearing company, combining international quality standards with local expertise and personalized service that our customers have come to depend on.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth as East Africa's leading bearing company

2008

Company Founded

M.L Bearing Solutions established in Nairobi with a focus on serving local manufacturing industries with quality bearing solutions.

2012

Regional Expansion

Extended operations to serve customers across Kenya, establishing partnerships with major industrial manufacturers and mining companies.

2016

Import Services Launch

Launched specialized import-on-request services, enabling customers to access any bearing specification from global manufacturers.

2019

East Africa Coverage

Achieved complete coverage of all 47 Kenyan counties and expanded services to Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda.

2024

Digital Innovation

Launched comprehensive digital platform and 24/7 customer support, becoming Africa's most accessible bearing solutions provider.
